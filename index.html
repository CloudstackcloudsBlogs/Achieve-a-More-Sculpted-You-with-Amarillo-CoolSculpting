What Is CoolSculpting and How Can It Benefit You?

CoolSculpting is a non-surgical procedure that targets and freezes fat cells using a technique called cryolipolysis. Approved by the FDA, this method selectively reduces fat by cooling fat cells to a temperature that causes their natural breakdown. Over time, the body naturally eliminates these dead cells, leaving behind a smoother and more contoured figure.

Our team at American Laser Med Spa – Amarillo is trained and certified to administer this procedure with the utmost care and precision. We focus on safety, comfort, and delivering visible results to each person who walks through our doors.

CoolSculpting is not intended as a weight-loss treatment. Instead, it is an effective body-contouring option for people who are close to their goal weight but struggle with localized fat deposits that don’t respond to traditional methods.

These zones are often resistant to exercise, and even a healthy diet may not yield noticeable improvements. That’s where CoolSculpting becomes an excellent option for reshaping your figure.

What to Expect During a CoolSculpting Session

When you arrive at American Laser Med Spa – Amarillo for your CoolSculpting session, we start with a thorough consultation. We’ll evaluate your target areas, discuss your goals, and tailor a personalized treatment plan.

Here’s a brief overview of what the treatment involves:

  1. Preparation: The skin is prepped, and a gel pad is placed to protect the surface.
  2. Application: The CoolSculpting applicator suctions the targeted area and begins delivering controlled cooling.
  3. Treatment Time: Each session typically lasts 35 to 60 minutes, depending on the area being treated.
  4. Comfort: Many of our clients read, work remotely, or even take a nap during treatment.

After the session, the treated area may appear red or feel numb for a short time. These effects are temporary and usually subside within hours or a few days.

Timeline and Results

Clients typically begin to notice changes in as little as three weeks, with the most noticeable results appearing between one and three months. The body continues to flush out fat cells for up to six months following treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CoolSculpting safe?

Yes. CoolSculpting is FDA-cleared and has been performed millions of times worldwide. Side effects are minimal and temporary.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on the area and the amount of fat being treated. Some clients achieve their desired results in one session, while others may benefit from two or more.

Will I lose weight with CoolSculpting?

CoolSculpting isn’t a weight-loss method. It’s best for people close to their ideal weight who want to reduce stubborn fat areas.

Is there downtime?

No. Most clients return to their regular activities immediately after treatment.

Does the fat come back?

The fat cells removed during CoolSculpting are gone permanently. However, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is key to keeping your new shape.
